What AnchorCast Does

Features Built for Church Services

Every feature designed for real Sunday mornings — from the first song to the closing prayer.

🎤
AI Powered
Live Bible Verse Detection
Listens to the sermon and automatically identifies Bible verses the instant they are spoken — direct quotes and paraphrased references. No operator input needed.
📖
Real-Time
Multi-Translation Bible
KJV bundled by default. Import NKJV, NIV, NLT, ESV, NASB, ASV or any translation via JSON. Switch mid-service. Verses auto-split into clean 2–4 line slides.
🎵
Free
Song Display & Manager
Full song library with lyrics editor and auto slide formatting. Import from EasyWorship or ProPresenter XML. Genius lyrics search. Verse, chorus, bridge auto-detected.
🖥
Multi-Screen
Multi-Monitor Projection
Operator previews on their screen while the congregation sees the live display. Drag the projection window to any connected display. Press F11 for fullscreen.
⏱
Standalone
Countdown & Count-Up Timer
On-screen timer with custom title, font, color, and position. Flashes red when time expires with a closing message. Also installs as a standalone AnchorCast Timer app.
📝
AI Powered
Sermon Transcript & AI Notes
Every sermon transcribed and auto-saved. Generate structured AI sermon notes with one click. Full searchable sermon archive by title, speaker, or verse reference.
📱
Remote
Wi-Fi Remote Control
Control from any phone or tablet. Prev, Next, Clear, Go Live — full control from anywhere. PIN-based access with separate roles for admin, scripture, songs, and media operators.
🎨
Free
Theme Designer & Slides
Custom fonts, colors, backgrounds, and logo overlays. Per-category themes for Scripture, Songs, and Presentations. 1920×1080 canvas-based slide editor built in.
🔴
Streaming
NDI & Live Streaming
Professional NDI output for OBS, vMix, and Wirecast. MJPEG fallback for browser-based clients. Logo overlays, nursery alerts, and live captions on the projection screen.
🔒
Offline
Works Without Internet
Local Whisper AI for transcription, local KJV Bible, local song library. AnchorCast works completely offline — no cloud dependency, no internet required on Sunday.
🧠
AI Powered
Adaptive Intelligence
Learns your preacher's vocabulary and accent over time to improve transcription accuracy. Custom vocabulary, per-speaker profiles, and correction rules built in.
📊
Free
Analytics & Archive
Full searchable archive of every service. Analytics across sessions — most-quoted books, recurring verses, keyword trends. Auto-generated post-service summaries.
